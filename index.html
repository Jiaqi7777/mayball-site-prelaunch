We're excited to announce that the Downing May Ball is coming back in 2023 on Tuesday 20th June! Follow us on Instagram and Facebook to keep up to date with the latest announcements.

Tickets

The ticket release dates have been announced! All tickets are available at noon on their respective release dates.
Downing (Students, Staff, Fellows, and Alumni) - 16.02.23
All Students/Alumni - 23.02.23


Guests will pay the same price as the person who invited them, except where access (or staff/fellow) discounts are applicable. Current Downing students, staff and fellows will be allowed 4 guests. All other ticket buyers may invite 1 guest. The guests do not need to buy the same ticket type as the person who invited them.

You may only purchase one add-on per standard entry ticket purchased. Add-on tickets do not provide entry to the ball and must be used in conjunction with a valid standard entry ticket.
Students who receive the full Cambridge bursary are eligible for reduced prices provided they send proof of their bursary status via email to ticketing@downingball.co.uk within 7 days of purchasing the ticket.

Proof can be provided via a picture of your student finance statement for this academic year, showing the maintenance loan amount. If you do not have access to this, any proof of payment of the termly bursary amount to your bank account by the university (e.g. via a picture of a bank statement which includes your name), may be used.

Guest Ticket Information


  • General policy

    You must arrive at the ball with your guest(s) and get your ticket scanned together.

  • Exception

    If, and only if, you and your guest(s) have different add-ons (i.e. you cannot arrive together) then you MUST email ticketing@downingball.co.uk before 28 May 2023 with the name(s) of your guest(s). This means they will be able to enter the ball without you present.

Name Changes


If you sell your ticket/give your ticket to somebody else, you must inform the ticketing officer via email at ticketing@downingball.co.uk.

Accessibility

Downing May Ball recognises the importance of accessibility for guests, workers, and vendors. We have published our provisional accessibility statement to give as much information about the accessibility of the event as we can at this point in time. We will publish a finalised accessibility statement nearer the event itself. If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions of how we can make the event and information about the event as accessible to you as possible, please get in touch with Eleanor on accessibility&welfare@downingball.co.uk. You are welcome to contact us even before purchasing a ticket or applying to work with us, and all information and communication between guests and our accessibility lead will be kept confidential (only to be shared with committee members on a need-to-know basis to implement accessible arrangements).
