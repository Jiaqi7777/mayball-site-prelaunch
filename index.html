The Return of the May Ball...



Employment

Downing May Ball is fortunate to work with a diverse range of providers. If you represent a UK-based company and would like to work with us to help create a fantastic student event for 2000+ guests in the heart of Cambridge, please register your interest below.

For Vendors

Downing May Ball will be looking to hire a great body of student workers to help make the night run smoothly. If you would like to be involved with one of the biggest and most elaborate May Week events, and be paid a competitive wage at the same time, please register your interest below. This is not an application, but will enable you to be the first to receive information about when applications will open!
